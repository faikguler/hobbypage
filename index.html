Chess

Chess is one of the most successful intelligence and strategy games invented by humanity. The way to play this strategy-based game well is to be a mentally active individual. A suitable environment is created for the development of a mentally active individual's abilities in this context. During the chess game, some cognitive skills such as working memory, fluent intelligence, attention, concentration, spatial positioning, metacognitive skills and the individual's ability to use these processes effectively develop.
